How does pricing work?

Your quote price is built in layers, starting with a base price calculated from your product's surface area.

From there, percentage increases are applied for things like thickness, grain direction, glue-ups, and complex patterns. Each percentage increase is calculated from the base price only, not from the running total. They are all added together and applied to the base price once.

Feature add-ons like juice grooves, finger holds, or custom engraving are fixed dollar amounts added after all percentage increases have been applied.

Labor and additional expenses are calculated separately and added to arrive at your final quote total.
